About Us

SENIMMO is now in the market entry stage, with a primary focus on offering an innovative solution to address the challenges renters face in Senegal. Our mission is to streamline the rent payment process and contribute to the development of a more digitally connected and environmentally sustainable society. The driving force behind SENIMMO is the desire to enhance convenience for tenants while reducing the environmental impact associated with traditional paper-based transactions.

Let us make live easier

Our vision is to be the leading platform empowering seamless and eco-friendly rent payments across Africa by 2035. We are starting this journey in Senegal.
